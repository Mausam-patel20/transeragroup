01Founder & CEO

Kapil Shah

MBA(C) · PMP · 309A·RSE · M.E. · C.Tech · DEE

A 20-year veteran of power, capital, and infrastructure delivery — with field-led experience across $100M+ portfolios and 1,500+ EV chargers deployed across 300+ sites in Canada and North America.

Kapil’s career bridges the trades and the boardroom. From hands-on electrical execution and high-voltage transmission programs to senior infrastructure leadership at one of Canada’s largest logistics enterprises, he has led complex, multi-stakeholder electrification programs from utility coordination through commissioning — consistently translating technical complexity into capital-efficient, governance-driven execution.

He founded Transera Group Ltd. to bring that same operating discipline — vendor-neutral, owner-side, capability-aligned — to the wider mid-market: fleets, real-estate portfolios, healthcare campuses, municipalities, indigenous and remote programs, and OEM/CPO partners building the next generation of charging infrastructure.

His credentials span Master Electrician (ESA, Ontario), Red Seal Endorsed Construction & Maintenance Electrician, OACETT Certified Engineering Technician, and PMP-led program governance — complemented by an MBA in Sustainability & Innovation currently in progress.

02Career Trajectory

Three decades of power, capital, and electrification.

From substations and transmission programs in India’s power-infrastructure boom, through high-voltage equipment supply across North America, to national-scale fleet electrification — a continuous arc of capital-grade infrastructure delivery.

Where it began

Kapil’s electrical infrastructure career began in childhood — his father was a General Electrical Contractor in Gujarat, India, who delivered high-value contracts for the Gujarat State Secretariat Building, Cancer Hospital, and other prestigious projects, alongside running an electrical goods distribution business across Gujarat. That household became the training ground.

03Sustainability Stance

Sustainability is part of how we think.

Electrification is fundamentally a sustainability act. Transera approaches every advisory engagement with sustainability awareness woven into the discussion — from grid impact and load planning to long-term lifecycle thinking. Where clients want structured ESG support, we coordinate it through the right specialist partners.

Our founder’s ongoing MBA in Sustainability & Innovation reinforces this lens — ensuring sustainability is treated as a strategic input to capital decisions, not a marketing afterthought.

04Professional Positioning

Owner-side advisory and EPCM coordination — delivered in collaboration with licensed professionals.

Transera Group Ltd. provides infrastructure strategy, governance frameworks, procurement oversight, construction coordination, and program-management leadership. Where regulated services are required, we collaborate with licensed Professional Engineers and Electrical Contractors engaged directly or in partnership.
