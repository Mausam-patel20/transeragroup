Home/Partnerships

An ecosystem built for delivery.

Transera operates as the Owner’s coordination layer — but every program is delivered with a network of licensed engineers, contractors, OEMs, and specialists. Our partner ecosystem is how we scale capability without scaling overhead.

01Partner Categories

Five tiers of trusted delivery partners.

We do not duplicate engineering scope. We integrate it. Our partner network is curated through working relationships across 20+ years of infrastructure delivery.

Tier 01 · Engineering

Professional Engineers

Sealed deliverables, regulated scope.

  • P.Eng-led electrical engineering
  • Civil & structural design partners
  • Substation & high-voltage specialists
  • Geotechnical & environmental consultants
Why it matters: Sealed engineering belongs with licensed P.Eng professionals — and we coordinate that relationship on the Owner’s behalf.
Tier 02 · Construction

Licensed Contractors

Regulated electrical & civil execution.

  • ECRA/ESA licensed electrical contractors
  • Master Electrician-led firms
  • Civil works & concrete contractors
  • Trenchless & underground specialists
Why it matters: Regulated electrical work in Ontario must be delivered by ECRA/ESA-licensed contractors. Transera coordinates & governs.
Tier 03 · Equipment

Charger & Equipment OEMs

Long-term lifecycle relationships.

  • Tier-1 EV charger manufacturers
  • Switchgear & transformer suppliers
  • Energy storage & battery system vendors
  • Software & charge management platforms
Why it matters: Vendor selection is a long-term commitment. We bring 20+ years of equipment evaluation experience.
Tier 04 · Utility & Authorities

Utility & AHJ Coordination

Connecting your project to the grid & jurisdiction.

  • Local distribution company (LDC) interfaces
  • Provincial transmission & system operators
  • Municipal building & permitting departments
  • Authorities Having Jurisdiction (AHJ)
  • Hydro One & IESO program coordination
Why it matters: Utility & AHJ delays are the #1 cause of EV charging program slippage. Pre-engagement is everything.
Tier 05 · Specialty

Specialty Consultants

Niche expertise, on-demand.

  • Commissioning & testing agencies
  • Cybersecurity & OT consultants
  • ESG & sustainability advisors
  • Insurance & surety specialists
  • Legal & contract advisors
Why it matters: Complex programs need specialist input — we know who to bring in, when.

02How We Engage Partners

Three relationship models — sized to context.

Transera works with partners through whichever structure best protects the Owner’s interests on a given program.

Owner-Direct

Transera coordinates. Owner contracts directly with each licensed partner. Maximum transparency, lowest cost.

Most common model

Joint Delivery

Transera + named lead partner co-deliver as a coordinated team. Single Owner-facing relationship, integrated billing.

For complex multi-discipline programs

Subcontracted Specialty

Transera engages specialists directly under our scope. Common for commissioning, ESG advisory, and AI tool buildouts.

For defined-scope work

03Strategic Advisors to Transera

Build Transera with us — on a mutually beneficial basis.

As Transera scales its electrification advisory practice, we are forming a curated bench of strategic advisors who help shape our business model, sharpen our delivery, and open doors. Hourly consulting and mutually beneficial engagement structures — designed to fit experienced industry leaders looking for selective, high-impact involvement.

Management

Management Advisor

Business strategy, scaling, governance, capital structure, and growth pathway for a founder-led advisory firm in expansion phase.

Ideal: experienced CEO / founder / GM with consulting or services-business scaling background.
Operations

Operations Advisor

Service delivery models, capacity planning, project-economics frameworks, client experience, and operating-system design.

Ideal: COO / Head of Delivery from professional services or EPCM consultancy.
Sustainability

Sustainability Advisor

ESG frameworks, GHG accounting fluency, sustainable procurement standards, and responsible-business positioning.

Ideal: sustainability lead, ESG analyst, or carbon-accounting practitioner with infrastructure exposure.
Technical

Technical Advisor

Engineering excellence, technology roadmap, software-platform direction, AI & digital-tools integration for the forthcoming Transera Toolbox.

Ideal: senior engineer, CTO, or technology-led product builder with utility / EV / power-systems expertise.
Commercial

Commercial Advisor

Pricing strategy, contract structures, business-development pipeline, and procurement-side relationship building.

Ideal: commercial leader, procurement officer, or BD lead from infrastructure / consulting domains.
Industry

Industry Advisor

Sector-specific expertise (Fleet, REITs, Healthcare, Municipal, OEMs, Indigenous & Remote) — matching delivery to vertical-specific norms.

Ideal: senior leader from a target industry vertical with electrification or capital-program exposure.
Legal & Compliance

Legal & Compliance Advisor

Regulatory positioning, contract templates, professional services compliance (PEO/ECRA/ESA boundaries), and risk frameworks.

Ideal: counsel with infrastructure / professional-services / regulatory background.
Finance

Finance & Capital Advisor

Capital planning for growth, financial modeling, treasury, and the eventual case for outside capital if and when needed.

Ideal: CFO, fractional finance leader, or investor-side professional.

Engagement structure: Strategic advisor relationships are typically structured as hourly consulting retainers, periodic advisory meetings, or mutually beneficial cross-referral arrangements. We invite a small number of advisors per role, prioritizing depth over breadth.
