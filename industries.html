09How We Engage

Three engagement models — sized to your program.

Every Owner is at a different point in the electrification pathway. Transera engages through whichever model fits your scope, scale, and decision-making rhythm. Pricing is structured to your scope and agreed in writing before work begins.

Project-Based Defined Scope

  • Single-site or single-program engagements with clearly defined deliverables
  • Fixed-fee or milestone-based billing tied to phase gates
  • Best for feasibility studies, readiness assessments, RFP support, or commissioning oversight
  • Typical timeline: 4 weeks to 6 months
  • Clear scope boundaries, written engagement letter, defined exit

Retainer-Based Ongoing

  • Multi-site portfolio coordination and program governance
  • Monthly engagement cadence with predictable hours allocation
  • Best for owner-side EPCM oversight across active rollouts
  • Typical term: 6 to 24 months with quarterly review checkpoints
  • Continuous capability access without project-by-project re-engagement

Embedded Advisory Strategic

  • Strategic advisor to executive leadership, board, or investment committee
  • Capital planning, capital governance, and capital allocation review
  • Best for organizations setting multi-year electrification strategy
  • Quarterly or board-cadence engagement with executive-grade reporting
  • Custom structures available — including fractional executive roles
