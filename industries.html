09How We Engage

Three engagement models — sized to context.

Most engagements start with a fixed-scope readiness assessment, then graduate to retainer or project-based delivery as the program scales.

Project-Based

  • Defined scope & deliverables
  • Fixed-fee or milestone billing
  • Best for single-site programs
  • Typical: $25K–$150K

Retainer-Based

  • Ongoing owner-side support
  • Multi-site portfolio coordination
  • Monthly engagement cadence
  • Typical: 6–24 month terms

Embedded Advisory

  • Strategic advisor to leadership
  • Capital planning & governance
  • Board / executive cadence
  • Custom structures available

All ranges are indicative. Final scope and structure are agreed in writing after a strategy call.
