About Transera

Bridging the Electrification Gap

Transera is an independent electrification advisory and EPCM services firm that supports small and mid-sized enterprises (SMEs) and public-sector organizations in delivering comprehensive EV electrical infrastructure programs.

As organizations accelerate electrification, many proceed without the necessary in-house engineering, procurement, construction management, or structured electrification governance capabilities. Projects frequently advance without validated feasibility, early utility alignment, phased deployment strategy, or disciplined execution oversight.

Transera bridges this capability gap through a structured, phased delivery model — beginning with the Electrification Readiness Blueprint and continuing through commissioning.

Core Capabilities

End-to-End EPCM Leadership

From Blueprint through commissioning, Transera provides end-to-end EPCM leadership and specialized consulting support — ensuring technical rigor, capital discipline, risk mitigation, and transparent governance across the full project lifecycle.

Strategic Blueprint

Fleet and depot capacity assessments, utility grid strategy, phased infrastructure planning, and capital modeling.

Engineering & Design

Full engineering and design coordination ensuring all aspects align with EV infrastructure deployment standards.

Procurement Governance

Vendor evaluation, procurement governance, cost management, schedule oversight, and risk mitigation throughout.

Commissioning & Closeout

Commissioning readiness, closeout governance, and operational handover for long-term performance.

Independent & Vendor-Neutral

Transera operates as an independent partner dedicated solely to owner interests — distinct from equipment suppliers or installation contractors. This allows organizations to confidently deploy EV charging infrastructure without extensive internal EPCM services. By merging structured electrification planning with disciplined EPCM execution, Transera facilitates scalable and resilient electrification programs.
