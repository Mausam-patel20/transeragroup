Home/Work With Us

Build the future of electrification with us.

Transera is a growing electrification advisory firm welcoming experienced professionals, advisors, and bright co-op and intern students who want to build their network and accelerate their knowledge in EV charging, capital infrastructure, and owner-side EPCM.

01Why Transera

A small team, with the leverage of a much larger one.

Mid-market electrification needs disciplined, right-sized advisory — and that is exactly the gap Transera fills. We’re building a structured, vendor-neutral firm that brings owner-side EPCM coordination to fleets, real estate portfolios, healthcare campuses, municipalities, indigenous programs, and OEM/CPO partners. It works because of the people we bring on.

You will work directly with the founder, on real programs, with real Owners. No bench time, no shadow assignments, no decks-for-the-deck’s-sake.

📐

Direct Owner Access

You’ll sit at the table with fleet managers, municipal directors, and OEM executives — not three layers below them.

🛠

Real Infrastructure

EV charging, transmission, substations, distribution. Programs that exist in the physical world, not slideware.

📈

Equity in Outcomes

As we grow, the people who shaped Transera early will share in what we build. Compensation models reflect that.

🌎

Geographic Flexibility

Headquartered in Ontario, but our programs are Canada-wide and increasingly North American.

02Roles We’re Building Toward

The people we’re looking for as we scale.

These aren’t open-tomorrow postings. They’re the disciplines we expect to bring on as we grow — if any of them describe you, we want to hear from you now.

Future Role

EPCM Project Lead

Field-led, governance-minded.

  • 10+ years EPCM or owner’s rep experience
  • EV charging or electrical infrastructure background
  • PMP or equivalent project governance
  • Strong utility coordination instincts
  • Comfortable in the field and at the boardroom
Engagement: Full-time or fractional. Toronto / Ontario-based preferred; remote considered.
Future Role

Sustainability & ESG Advisor

Avoided emissions, ESG disclosure, sustainability strategy.

  • 5+ years sustainability advisory or corporate ESG
  • GHG accounting fluency (Scope 1, 2, 3)
  • Familiarity with TCFD, SASB, GRI frameworks
  • Comfort translating ESG to capital decisions
  • MBA-Sustainability or equivalent training preferred
Engagement: Project-based or fractional retainer.
Future Role

BD & Client Strategy

Pipeline development across fleet, SME, municipal.

  • 5+ years B2B advisory or infrastructure sales
  • Existing network in Canadian fleet, public sector, or utilities
  • Comfortable opening conversations with C-suite Owners
  • Strategic, not transactional
  • Open to commission-weighted compensation
Engagement: Part-time, advisor, or full-time. Performance-based structures available.
Future Role

Engineering Coordinator

Owner-side technical liaison.

  • Engineering technologist or junior P.Eng background
  • Familiarity with EV charging, switchgear, or substation work
  • Strong document-control and review habits
  • OACETT, EIT, or P.Eng standing
Engagement: Full-time or hybrid. Excellent learning role for someone scaling into EPCM leadership.
Future Role

Operations & AI Tools

Build the tooling that makes us scale.

  • Background in software, data, or automation
  • Comfortable with AI tools, prompt engineering, integrations
  • Interest in infrastructure / energy / climate sectors
  • Self-directed; works well with non-technical stakeholders
Engagement: Part-time, contractor, or fractional CTO model.

03Co-op & Intern Students

Build your network. Accelerate your knowledge.

We welcome bright co-op and intern students from electrical engineering, project management, sustainability, business, and digital tools backgrounds who want hands-on exposure to real-world electrification programs.

Engineering & Project Co-ops

Electrical, civil, mechanical, or project management students looking for capital-infrastructure exposure. You’ll see real Owner conversations, real utility coordination, and real EPCM workflows.

  • Conestoga, Humber, Mohawk, OACETT-affiliated programs
  • University co-op programs (UWaterloo, Western, McMaster, Toronto)
  • 4-month or 8-month placements

Business & Sustainability Interns

MBA, undergraduate business, sustainability, or policy students who want to learn how strategic advisory firms operate. Direct exposure to capital structuring, ESG framing, and client-facing engagements.

  • MBA programs across Canada
  • Sustainability & ESG-focused undergrad programs
  • Project work or summer internships

Digital & AI Tools Interns

Computer science, data science, or design students excited about building decision-support tools for capital infrastructure. The forthcoming Transera Toolbox is a perfect playground.

  • CS / data science programs
  • UX / product design programs
  • Project work or remote-friendly placements

Why Transera for co-ops & interns: You will work directly with the founder. You will see how owner-side advisory works in practice. You will build your professional network through Transera’s industry connections (EMC, EV Society, OACETT, PMI, conference circuit). And you’ll graduate with portfolio-grade work product.

04How to Apply

One email, one resume, one conversation.

Whether you’re applying for a future advisor role or for a co-op / internship placement, the entry point is the same: one email with your resume and a short note.

What to Send Required

  • Resume / CV attached as PDF
  • Your name and the role or co-op type that fits
  • 2–3 sentences on why Transera
  • One program type you’d want to contribute to
  • LinkedIn profile (optional but encouraged)
  • Any constraints (location, full-time vs fractional, timing, school program for co-ops)

What Happens Next Process

  • Founder reads every application personally
  • For experienced roles: 30-min introductory call if there’s a fit
  • For co-ops/interns: brief video call to align on goals & placement scope
  • Transparent discussion of scope, structure, and compensation
  • If timing isn’t right, we keep your note on file with permission
Email Application to contact@transeragroup.com

Attach your resume as a PDF · Subject line: “Work With Us Application” or “Co-op Application — [your school]”

Two-Way Match

The right role finds the right person.

If you read this far and felt something resonate — that’s usually a signal worth following. Send your resume to contact@transeragroup.com and we’ll be in touch.
