01Advisory

Strategic Advisory

Strategic structuring before engineering, procurement, or construction begins. We help Owners build the capital case, validate site readiness, model ROI, and set the program up for disciplined delivery.

Most overruns originate before a single drawing is sealed. Advisory work is where programs are won or lost — and where Transera adds the most value.

Site Readiness Assessment

Electrical capacity, civil constraints, utility coordination posture, and site logistics — captured before procurement begins.

Capital Roadmaps

Phased deployment plans, budget envelopes, and capital allocation frameworks aligned to operational priorities.

ROI & Business Case Modeling

ICE-vs-EV TCO analysis, demand-charge modeling, and incentive optimization across North America.

Vendor & OEM Evaluation

Charger selection frameworks, software-stack evaluation, and supplier diligence designed for long-term lifecycle fit.

Coordination for Design and/or Engineering

Owner-side oversight of P.Eng partners — single-line diagrams, load calcs, civil & structural integration, sealed deliverables.

Procurement & Contracts

RFP packages, vendor diligence, contract structuring, long-lead equipment strategy, and milestone-based payment governance.

Construction Governance

Owner’s rep on-site or remote; quality oversight, change-control discipline, schedule realism, and contractor accountability.

Commissioning & Handover

Performance verification, documentation compliance, operational readiness, and clean handover to operations teams.

02EPC Coordination

EPC Coordination

Coordination for Design and/or Engineering, procurement, and construction management coordinated from the Owner’s side of the table. Sealed engineering and licensed electrical contracting are delivered through P.Eng and ECRA/ESA partners, while Transera coordinates the full ecosystem on your behalf.

You get discipline, accountability, and a single point of governance — right-sized for your context and your capital.

03Program Management

Program Management

Multi-site, multi-stakeholder program governance built for portfolios that span jurisdictions, utilities, and operating units. PMI-grade controls, executive-grade reporting.

When you’re running 10, 50, or 200 sites — coordination becomes the binding constraint. Transera builds the controls and reporting that make portfolio-scale delivery possible.

Program Controls

Cost, schedule, risk, and change-control frameworks designed for multi-site portfolios with diverse stakeholders.

Multi-Site Rollout Coordination

Sequenced deployment plans, resource leveling, parallel-tracking, and integrated logistics across geographies.

Executive Reporting

Board-grade dashboards, milestone reporting, financial variance analysis, and benefits-realization narratives for sponsors.

Operations & Asset Management

Post-commissioning performance tracking, utilization analytics, maintenance coordination, and lifecycle asset planning.

04Engagement Model

Clear lines protect Owners.

A defined engagement model is the foundation of disciplined delivery. Transera operates within a specific advisory, EPCM coordination, and licensed-contracting scope — with regulated and specialised work delivered through partners. This clarity protects your program commercially, technically, and legally.

Delivered Directly by Transera

What We Will Provide

Core advisory, governance, and EPCM coordination services delivered directly by Transera Group Ltd. Once Transera’s ECRA/ESA Electrical Contractor Registration is active, licensed electrical contracting services within the registered scope are delivered in-house.

Owner-Side Advisory & Strategy

Independent guidance on electrification roadmap, program structuring, capital phasing, and decision support before and during execution.

EPCM Coordination Leadership

End-to-end engineering, procurement, construction management coordination — including vendor governance, scope packaging, and risk oversight.

Program & Portfolio Governance

Stage-gate frameworks, milestone reporting, executive dashboards, and disciplined delivery oversight across multi-site programs.

Procurement Structuring & Oversight

RFP design, vendor evaluation frameworks, contract structuring, and procurement governance — without holding equipment-resale incentives.

Risk & Benefits-Realisation Frameworks

Risk register design, mitigation tracking, and structured benefits-realisation models linking infrastructure decisions to operational outcomes.

Stakeholder & Executive Reporting

Board-level program reports, utility coordination updates, regulatory progress documentation, and transparent stakeholder communication.

Delivered Through Partners

What We Provide With Partners

Specialised work delivered through Transera’s vetted partner network — engaged by Transera under a coordinated scope that protects the Owner’s commercial and technical interests.

P.Eng-Stamped Engineering

Licensed Professional Engineers engaged via retainer or contract basis, providing sealed engineering deliverables for civil, electrical, structural, and protection & control scopes when regulatory submissions require them.

Licensed Electrical Sub-Contracting

Licensed ECRA/ESA Electrical Contractors engaged on a project-by-project basis to execute electrical installation, commissioning, and certification work outside Transera’s direct license scope.

Niche Electrical Equipment & Spares

Specialised electrical equipment, replacement spares, and hard-to-source components supplied through trusted vendor relationships, with neutral procurement structuring that prioritises Owner interests.

EV Charging Monitoring & Asset Management Platform

Customised monitoring and asset-management platform configurations for clients managing large EV charging fleets — delivered with software partners and tailored to operational reporting needs.

Geotechnical, Environmental & Civil Studies

Geotechnical investigations, environmental assessments, and civil/structural design coordinated through specialised consulting partners as project requirements demand.

Commissioning, Testing & AHJ Submissions

Independent commissioning agencies, electrical testing services, and Authority Having Jurisdiction submissions coordinated through certified partners.

Explicit Exclusions

Transera Group Ltd. does not provide regulated financial advice, investment recommendations, tax planning, or legal counsel. Where such guidance is needed for an electrification program, Transera helps Owners engage qualified third-party professionals.

Disclaimer language regarding regulated professional services is detailed in the footer of this site and in every Transera engagement letter.
