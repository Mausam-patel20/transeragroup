01Advisory

Strategic Advisory

Strategic structuring before engineering, procurement, or construction begins. We help Owners build the capital case, validate site readiness, model ROI, and set the program up for disciplined delivery.

Most overruns originate before a single drawing is sealed. Advisory work is where programs are won or lost — and where Transera adds the most value.

Site Readiness Assessment

Electrical capacity, civil constraints, utility coordination posture, and site logistics — captured before procurement begins.

Capital Roadmaps

Phased deployment plans, budget envelopes, and capital allocation frameworks aligned to operational priorities.

ROI & Business Case Modeling

ICE-vs-EV TCO analysis, demand-charge modeling, and incentive optimization across North America.

Vendor & OEM Evaluation

Charger selection frameworks, software-stack evaluation, and supplier diligence designed for long-term lifecycle fit.

Engineering Coordination

Owner-side oversight of P.Eng partners — single-line diagrams, load calcs, civil & structural integration, sealed deliverables.

Procurement & Contracts

RFP packages, vendor diligence, contract structuring, long-lead equipment strategy, and milestone-based payment governance.

Construction Governance

Owner’s rep on-site or remote; quality oversight, change-control discipline, schedule realism, and contractor accountability.

Commissioning & Handover

Performance verification, documentation compliance, operational readiness, and clean handover to operations teams.

02EPC Coordination

EPC Coordination

Engineering, procurement, and construction management coordinated from the Owner’s side of the table. Sealed engineering and licensed electrical contracting are delivered through P.Eng and ECRA/ESA partners, while Transera coordinates the full ecosystem on your behalf.

You get discipline, accountability, and a single point of governance — right-sized for your context and your capital.

03Program Management

Program Management

Multi-site, multi-stakeholder program governance built for portfolios that span jurisdictions, utilities, and operating units. PMI-grade controls, executive-grade reporting.

When you’re running 10, 50, or 200 sites — coordination becomes the binding constraint. Transera builds the controls and reporting that make portfolio-scale delivery possible.

Program Controls

Cost, schedule, risk, and change-control frameworks designed for multi-site portfolios with diverse stakeholders.

Multi-Site Rollout Coordination

Sequenced deployment plans, resource leveling, parallel-tracking, and integrated logistics across geographies.

Executive Reporting

Board-grade dashboards, milestone reporting, financial variance analysis, and benefits-realization narratives for sponsors.

Operations & Asset Management

Post-commissioning performance tracking, utilization analytics, maintenance coordination, and lifecycle asset planning.

04Scope Boundaries

Clear lines protect Owners.

A defined scope is the foundation of disciplined delivery. Transera operates within a specific advisory and EPCM coordination scope — with explicit boundaries on regulated services, which are delivered by licensed partners. This clarity protects your program commercially, technically, and legally.

Within Scope

What Transera Delivers Directly

  • Owner-side advisory & strategy
  • EPCM coordination leadership
  • Program & portfolio governance
  • Procurement structuring & oversight
  • Risk & benefits-realization frameworks
  • Stakeholder & executive reporting
Coordinated With Partners

Delivered Via Licensed Specialists

  • Sealed engineering (P.Eng partners)
  • Licensed electrical contracting (ECRA/ESA)
  • Civil & structural design
  • Geotechnical & environmental studies
  • Commissioning & testing agencies
  • Permitting & AHJ submissions
Outside Scope

What Transera Does Not Do

  • Sealed P.Eng deliverables in-house
  • Licensed electrical contracting in-house
  • Equipment manufacturing or resale
  • Software product development
  • Regulated financial advice
  • Legal counsel
